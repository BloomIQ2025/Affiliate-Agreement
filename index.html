Lantern Lead Gen Affiliate Agreement

Lantern Lead Gen

Effective Date: [Insert Date]
This Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into by and between Lantern Lead Gen, a lead generation service (“Company”), and the undersigned content creator or affiliate (“Affiliate”).
________________________________________
I. Appointment and Scope of Services
Affiliate agrees to promote custom-branded lead intake pages created by Lantern Lead Gen to their audience via TikTok or other approved platforms. The Company will track leads generated through the Affiliate’s unique link and compensate the Affiliate based on qualified lead revenue.
________________________________________
II. Services Provided by Lantern Lead Gen
Lantern Lead Gen agrees to:
• Design and host a custom lead intake page using the Affiliate’s branding (colors, slogans, imagery).
• Track and attribute leads generated through the Affiliate’s unique campaign link.
• Deliver qualified leads to certified life coaches.
• Provide real-time or periodic reporting on lead performance and earnings.
________________________________________
III. Affiliate Responsibilities
The Affiliate agrees to:
• Promote the lead intake page in good faith and in alignment with the Company’s brand values.
• Avoid misleading, deceptive, or coercive tactics in content or messaging.
• Disclose affiliate relationship in accordance with FTC guidelines (e.g., “I may earn a commission…”).
• Not incentivize submissions (e.g., giveaways, contests) without prior written approval.
• Not use paid ads, bots, or spam to drive traffic to the intake page.
• Comply with all applicable platform rules and advertising standards.
________________________________________
IV. Compensation
• The Affiliate will receive 70% of net profits generated from leads submitted through their unique link.
• Payments will be issued monthly via PayPal, Venmo, or another agreed-upon method.
• A detailed earnings report will accompany each payment.
• The Company reserves the right to withhold payment for fraudulent or non-compliant leads.
________________________________________
V. Data Handling and Privacy
• The Affiliate will not have access to lead data submitted through the intake page.
• All data is collected and stored securely by Lantern Lead Gen in accordance with its Privacy Policy.
• The Affiliate agrees not to request, store, or misuse any personal data related to leads.
________________________________________
VI. Compliance and Monitoring
• The Company may submit test (“dummy”) leads to monitor compliance.
• Affiliates found to be in violation of this Agreement or applicable laws may have their campaigns paused or terminated.
